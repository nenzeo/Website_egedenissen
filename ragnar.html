About me

As a person whos Always eager to learn and develop new skills,
being thrown into various project as a Systems Consultant in the IT world back in 2008,
suited me perfect.

I consider myself as a hardworking individual who works good in teams as well as on my own.

After a couple of years as a Consultant,
which included system architecture, sales, sysadmin, networking and onsite support.
I wanted to specialize within the industry.
So i moved onto my next adventure,
still in IT but specializing in the technicalities of client computers,
as well as leading teams in "conversion" projects. Working with loads of projects for different companies, clients and new customers,
finding ways of maximizing my teams efficiency, was one of my main goals.

Throughout my years of experience, i have developed many skills,
and working under high pressure, deadlines and different platforms,
have strengthened my eye for details,
and not to mention a good understanding of the industry.

I learn fast, and am not afraid to find and explore original ways of learning.
Learning by doing has definitely been one of my strengths throughout life.

For the last two years I have become especially fond of learning the intricacies of programming languages.
Just recently finished learning Chef, and am at the moment studying the Ruby language to compliment Chef.

I always enjoy a good challenge.

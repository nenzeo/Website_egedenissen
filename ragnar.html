About me

Hey, I am a 32 year old IT guy living in Oslo with my wife and daughter. In my first IT job I was thrown into rather large projects as a Systems Consultant. With my head well beneath the waterline I managed to pull the projects together with the help of some search engines and some contacts. My next IT job gave me access to leading teams in "conversion" project. I would get sparse information about a new customers in the weeks leading up to a weekend and when the workday on Friday was over the team would have until Monday morning to finish the conversion. Gradually learning a client has twice the amount of Users, hidden systems like door controls, factory automation tied into IT systems and super specialized printers from the 80s, can really put fear in someone responsible. Since those jobs I have relished the challenge of the new and difficult and with the years my experience has grown wide, making me able to tackle a lot of different challenges. In my sparetime I have many hobbies; gaming, woodworking, leatherwoking, restorations, robotics, programming, 3D modelling and game asset creation(modding).
